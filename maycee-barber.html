Professional Career

After winning one amateur fight, Maycee began fighting professionally at the age of 19. She won in less than 20 seconds. She later became a member of the Legacy Fighting Alliance (LFA). UFC career

She made her debut fight with UFC on November 10, 2018, on UFC Fight Night: Korean Zombie vs. Rodríguez against Hannah Cifers. She won the fight by technical knockout (TKO) in the second round. Similarly, she fought Gillian Robertson at UFC on ESPN 6 on October 18, 2019. She won the fight again, this time by technical knockout in the first round. She then fought Roxanne Modafferi at UFC 246 on January 18, 2020. Unfortunately, she was defeated by a unanimous decision. In this fight, she lost for the first time in her professional career.
