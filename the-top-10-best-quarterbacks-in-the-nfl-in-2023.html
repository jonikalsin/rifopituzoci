The top 10 Best Quarterbacks in the NFL in 2023

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, August 9, 2024

In the NFL, having an elite quarterback is often seen as the key to success. The quarterback position is arguably the most important on the field, as they are responsible for leading the offense and making critical decisions. A talented quarterback can elevate their team's performance and increase their chances of winning championships.

Recent Success of MVP-Caliber Quarterbacks

Recent Super Bowl winners have showcased the importance of having an MVP-caliber quarterback. Patrick Mahomes, the Kansas City Chiefs' quarterback, led his team to victory in Super Bowl LIV and was named the game's MVP. His ability to make exceptional throws under pressure and extend plays with his mobility has set him apart as one of the league's top quarterbacks.

The Role of Supporting Cast

While having an elite quarterback is crucial, it is also important to acknowledge the role of a strong supporting cast. A talented group of receivers, a solid offensive line, and a competent coaching staff can elevate a quarterback's performance and lead to success. This is evident in teams like the Kansas City Chiefs and the Miami Dolphins, who have surrounded their quarterbacks with talented playmakers.

The Top 10 Quarterbacks in the NFL in 2023

At this point in the 2023 NFL season, several quarterbacks stand out as the best in the league. These players possess a combination of talent, skill, and leadership that sets them apart from their peers. Let's take a closer look at the top 10 quarterbacks in the NFL in 2023.

1. Patrick Mahomes

Patrick Mahomes is widely regarded as one of the most talented quarterbacks in the NFL. His ability to make throws from different arm angles, his arm strength, and his uncanny ability to extend plays make him a nightmare for opposing defenses. Mahomes' creativity and ability to improvise have made him a two-time Super Bowl participant and a former Super Bowl MVP.

2. Josh Allen

Josh Allen has proven himself to be a great quarterback due to his remarkable improvement over the years. Despite facing skepticism and doubts about his abilities, Allen has consistently shown growth and progress in his performance. He has significantly increased his completion percentage and has been performing at an elite level, drawing comparisons to top quarterbacks like Tom Brady. Allen's dedication, work ethic, and ability to adapt and improve have been key factors in his success as a great quarterback.

3.Dak Prescott

Dak Prescott, despite his high interception total in 2022, is expected to perform well in 2023 with a new offensive coordinator. His strong arm, mobility, and leadership skills make him a dangerous quarterback. Prescott's ability to make plays with his legs and deliver accurate throws downfield has made him a valuable asset to the Dallas Cowboys.

4.Jalen Hurts

Jalen Hurts has shown flashes of brilliance during his time as the Philadelphia Eagles' quarterback. His mobility, arm strength, and leadership skills have made him a promising young talent. Hurts' ability to make plays with his legs and deliver accurate throws downfield make him an exciting player to watch.

5. Lamar Jackson

Lamar Jackson's athleticism and speed have made him a unique threat at the quarterback position. His ability to make explosive plays with his legs has kept defenses on their toes. Jackson's improved passing ability and decision-making have made him a more complete quarterback, and he continues to be a dynamic playmaker for the Baltimore Ravens.

6. Justin Herbert

Justin Herbert burst onto the scene as a rookie in 2020 and has continued to impress. His arm strength, accuracy, and poise under pressure have made him one of the most promising young quarterbacks in the league. Herbert's ability to make difficult throws and lead his team in critical moments has earned him a spot among the league's top quarterbacks.

7.Joe Burrow

Joe Burrow has quickly established himself as a great quarterback due to his exceptional poise, accuracy, and decision-making on the field. His ability to read defenses, make quick adjustments, and deliver pinpoint passes has been instrumental in his success. Burrow's leadership skills, football intelligence, and competitive drive have made him a natural leader, earning the respect of his teammates and coaches alike. Unfortunately he is not higher up on the list due to getting injured earlier in the season.

8. Trevor Lawrence

Trevor Lawrence, the first overall pick in the 2021 NFL Draft, has immense potential. His arm talent, size, and football IQ have made him a highly touted prospect. Lawrence's ability to make all the throws and his poise in the pocket make him a promising young quarterback for the Jacksonville Jaguars.

9. Tua Tagovailoa

Tua Tagovailoa is a great quarterback due to his exceptional accuracy and ability to make precise throws, threading the needle even in tight coverage. His poise under pressure is remarkable, as he remains composed in the pocket and makes smart decisions with the football. Tua's leadership skills and football intelligence also contribute to his greatness, as he consistently demonstrates a deep understanding of the game and the ability to lead his team to victory. Tue does have a lot of help as he does have one of the best receiving cores in the NFL with Tyreek Hill and Jaylen Waddle.

10. CJ Stroud

CJ Stroud, the Texans' rookie quarterback, possesses a remarkable combination of arm strength, accuracy, and poise in the pocket. His ability to make quick decisions, read defenses, and deliver precise passes sets him apart from his peers. Stroud's work ethic, dedication to his craft, and natural talent make him a riser on this list and we are excited to see him in the top 5 in 2024.

Having an elite quarterback is crucial to a team's success in the NFL. The top 10 quarterbacks in the league in 2023 possess a combination of talent, skill, and leadership that sets them apart. Left off the list are names like Brock Purdy who has had an amazing season with the 49ers and Aaron Rodgers who was hurt on one of the 1st plays of the season unfortunately. While individual talent is important, a strong supporting cast can elevate a quarterback's performance and increase their chances of winning championships. The 2023 season promises to be an exciting one, with these quarterbacks leading the way.
