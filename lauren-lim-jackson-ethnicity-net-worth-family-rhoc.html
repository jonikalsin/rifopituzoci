Lauren Lim Jackson Ethnicity, Net Worth, Married, Height

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, June 6, 2024

Acting coach Lauren Lim Jackson returned on television, on The Real Housewives of Orange County, and her fans could not help but admire her.

The RHOC viewers were for the first time introduced to Lauren in an earlier episode of Season 17. Now, because people are loving her so much, we thought we should also get to know her better.

Lauren Lim Jackson On Real Housewives Of Orange County

RHOC fans recently have taken so much liking to Lauren Lim Jackson that they thought they would try calling out Bravo for the lack of diversity on the show. After seeing Lauren on the show, they took to the internet to gush over the addition to the cast. Bravo needs to cast Lauren on the season, they gave their suggestion. Another well wishes of Lauren’s pointed they should start a petition to have her permanently added to the series.

As for Lauren, she still had not announced plans to officially join the show, as of July 2023.

Season 17 of Bravo’s show premiered on 7 June 2023 and so far is known to star the OGs Shannon Storms Beador, Heather Dubrow, Gina Kirschenheiter, Tamra Judge, and Emily Simpson.

How Much Is Lauren Lim Jackson’s Net Worth?

Lauren Lim Jackson reportedly had a $2 million-plus net worth as of July 2023.

Lauren is the co-founder and CEO of LVOE Productions, an entertainment production company created alongside Vince Oddo.

She has had an entertainment industry career that spans over 20 years. Not long ago, she produced alongside Award winning Production Company WalkRunFly (Hunger Games Live) on the new musical Part of the Plan, penned by Kate Atkinson and Daytime Emmy Award-winning writer Karen Harris.

Lauren, at the same time, has also been professionally in Broadway, Television, Film, and Stage. Her Film/TV credits include Almost Family, American Soul, High Maintenance, The Blacklist, When We Grow Up, Shades of Blue, Daredevil, The Smurfs, and Broadway credits include Pretty Woman, Motown, Memphis, Wonderland, and Finian’s Rainbow to name a few.

You should also know that Lauren is a Harvard University graduate with a B.A. in Sociology and a Secondary degree in Women, Gender, and Sexuality Studies.

Lauren Lim Jackson’s Age

Lauren Lim was born in 1985. So, she reached the age of 38 in 2023.

What Is Lauren Lim Jackson’s Ethnicity?

Lauren Lim Jackson is of Chinese, Filipino, and African American descent. She is really proud of her heritage. She can be seen celebrating the likes of Asian/Pacific Islander American Month and advocating representation on Broadway.

Lauren says the importance of giving back was instilled in her at a young age. She loves to tell the story of how as a twelve-year-old she was working alongside her family at soup kitchens, hospitals, and shelters.

Lauren Lim Jackson Family

Lauren Lim Jackson grew up in Lake Forest, where her parents are based even today. Recalling finding her love for performing early, Lauren mentioned having an older sister and said she did whatever her sibling did.

Lauren has also talked about a time in her life, after a few years at Harvard, when she just wanted to quit and pursue her Broadway dreams. She said her father had nixed that plan but agreed that once she graduated, she could do what she wanted. Later, with a degree in hand, she moved to New York.

Lauren’s mother is Tunina L Jackson and she turned 72 in October 2022. Often Lauren has thanked her beautiful mom for showing her the best ways to be a mother. As for Lauren’s dad Laurence E Jackson, he turned 73 in December of that year. Both the folks are still together, married, and have been even seemingly enjoying each other’s company.

Lauren’s sister, Bermina Beatrice Jackson, is a self-proclaimed “very nice person”. She is from Los Angeles and she reached the age of 43 in May 2023. Also, she is an actress, known for Beautiful (2000), A Time for Dancing (2002), and The Looney Wizard of OZ (1987).

Also, this man named Edward L Jackson, of Lake Forest, California is most likely Lauren’s brother.

Lauren Lim Jackson Height

Lauren Lim Jackson stands below 5’8” in height.

Is Lauren Lim Jackson Married?

Indeed, Lauren Lim Jackson is married. She has been pledged to her husband Vincent I Oddo since 28 September 2013. On this day lately, Lauren was seen telling her beloved “Whenever I’m able to catch a glance of your glance at me, I’m undeniably reminded that I am Safe, that I am Supported, and that I am LOVED.” Even today, Lauren is confident that she would not do this life with anyone else or have it any other way.

Vincent also is an actor. He made his New York acting debut in Rocky on Broadway. Since then, he appeared on Broadway countless times with the last appearance co-starring in Michael Moore’s The Terms of My Surrender.

So, being actors, Lauren and her husband had originally planned to move to L.A. But, after spending time with the former’s parents in Lake Forest during the pandemic, they decided to remain just there with their kids. They especially wanted their daughter to spend time with her grandparents and Lauren’s sister’s daughter.

The couple have two more kids. Just on 18 June 2023, Lauren on her Instagram revealed each one’s names and birth years as she wished her husband a ‘Happy Father’s Day’.

So, the couple’s kids are Dominica Lim (b. 2017), Quinnton Bessie-James (b. 2021), and Walter Laurence (b. 2023).
