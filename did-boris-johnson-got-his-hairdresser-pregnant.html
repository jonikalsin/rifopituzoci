Did Boris Johnson Got His Hairdresser Pregnant? Cheating Rumors Escalate Amid Appearances With Wife

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, June 4, 2024

While attending the University of Oxford, Boris Johnson met Allegra Mostyn-Owen, and the two later married. The daughter of art historian William Mostyn-Owen and Italian author Gaia Sevadio, Mostyn-Owen was born in England.

The couple never had children; hence their marriage terminated in divorce in 1993.

On May 8, 1993, barely 12 days after his first divorce, Boris Johnson wed barrister Marina Wheeler. Lara Lettice, Milo Arthur, Cassia Peaches, and Theodore Apollo are the couple’s four children.

Following Johnson’s alleged infidelity with his now-wife Carrie Johnson, the couple announced their separation in 2018.

In November 2001, rumors of an adulterous relationship with journalist Petronella Wyatt led to Boris Johnson’s forced resignation from his role as shadow minister for the arts.

According to reports, Carrie Johnson, who oversaw communications for the Conservative Party, met Boris Johnson there.

The couple turned out to be the first unmarried couple to dwell at the Prime Minister’s mansion after getting engaged in late 2019. The pair got legally married in May 2021 and have two children.
