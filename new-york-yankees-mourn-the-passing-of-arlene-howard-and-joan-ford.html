New York Yankees Mourn the Passing of Arlene Howard and Joan Ford

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, June 6, 2024

The New York Yankees community is mourning the loss of two iconic women, Arlene Howard and Joan Ford. Arlene, the cherished wife of trailblazing player Elston Howard, and Joan, the devoted wife of legendary pitcher Whitey Ford, have left an indelible mark on the Yankees family.

Pillars of Strength and Grace

Both Arlene Howard and Joan Ford were known for their steadfast support of their husbands’ careers. Arlene’s presence at Yankees Old-Timers’ Days was a poignant reminder of her husband’s groundbreaking journey as the first African American player for the New York Yankees. Similarly, Joan’s unwavering support of Whitey Ford, one of the greatest pitchers in baseball history, has been well-documented.

The Yankees mourn the loss of the wives of two Yankees legends, Joan Ford, wife of Whitey Ford, and Arlene Howard, the wife of Elston Howard. Both women were guests of honor at Yankees Old-Timers’ Days and carried on the legacies of their husbands. The Yankees offer our heartfelt condolences to the Ford and Howard families.

Cause of Death

While specific details regarding the cause of Arlene Howard’s and Joan Ford’s deaths have not been disclosed, it is known that both women passed away peacefully, surrounded by their families.

Tributes Pour In

The news of Arlene Howard’s and Joan Ford’s passing has led to an outpouring of heartfelt tributes from the Yankees community and beyond. They are remembered not just as the wives of Yankees legends but also as beloved figures in their own right. Their grace, strength, and the love they had for their family and the Yankees community will forever be cherished.

Conclusion

The passing of Arlene Howard and Joan Ford marks the end of an era. As stalwart supporters of their husbands and integral members of the Yankees family, their memory will continue to inspire many. The Yankees community extends their deepest condolences to the Howard and Ford families during this difficult time.
