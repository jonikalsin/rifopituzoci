Jessica Pare is a Canadian actress and singer who is best known for portraying roles such as Megan Calvet Draper in Mad Men, Officer Amanda “Mandy” Ellis in SEAL Team, Courtney Benedict in Jack & Bobby, and Victoria “Tori” Moller in Lost and Delirious. She has also appeared in a variety of other TV shows and films such as Possible Worlds, See This Movie, Hot Tub Time Machine, The Trotsky, Another Kind of Wedding, Death of a Ladies’ Man, The Baby-Sitters Club, Satisfaction, The Interestings, Robot Chicken, The Simpsons, Atypical, and many more. As a singer, she has released singles like Zou Bisou Bisou.
