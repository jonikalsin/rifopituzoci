Kane Brown is an American singer known for his hit singles like What Ifs, Used to Love You Sober, Last Minute Late Night, I Love That I Hate You, and Ain’t No Stopping Us Now, and Lose It. He won the CMT Music Award for “Collaborative Video of the Year” for his hit single “What Ifs” featuring Lauren Alaina in 2018. He has more than 4 million followers on Instagram and more than 2.5 million followers on Twitter. On YouTube, he has more than 4 million subscribers.

Girlfriend / Spouse

Kane Brown has dated –

  • Katelyn Jae (2015-Present) – In 2015, Kane started dating music management student and fellow singer Katelyn Jae. They met through a typical work associate. The couple kept their relationship secret from the media. It was only in January 2017 that Kane shared his relationship status with his fans on Instagram. Later, in April 2017 during a concert in Philadelphia, he announced their engagement. The couple finally tied the knot at Mint Springs Farm in Nolensville, Tennessee amidst 200 guests, on October 12, 2018. On October 29, 2019, the couple welcomed their first child, daughter Kingsley Rose Brown. In December 2021, the couple revealed that they had welcomed their 2nd child together, a baby girl named Kodi Jane Brown. In December 2023, the couple revealed that they were expecting their 3rd child together.
