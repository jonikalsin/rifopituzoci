Sadly, Annie Wersching, a famous Hollywood actress, is no longer with us. At the age of 45, the Timeless star passed away. After a fight with cancer, she passed away. Her husband Stephen Full announced the devastating news of her passing.

2009 saw the marriage of Annie and Stephen, who she adores. For more information on Stephen Full and his marriage to 24 actress Annie Wersching, keep reading.

Stephen Full is an actor and comedian

Yes, it is what you read. Stephen is a comedian and actor, thus he is well-versed in the entertainment industry. On November 13, 1969, he was born in Chicago, Illinois. He is 53 years old right now.

Stephen has had a long career acting in both films and television programs. He is most known for his performance in I’m in the Band as Ash and for lending his voice to the Disney Channel series Dog with a Blog as Stan.

Stephen also played the part of Benny in the 2009 television series Castle. He appeared in two episodes of Hannah Montana on the Disney Channel and two episodes of Las Vegas on NBC. Additionally, he has appeared in other shows like Best Friends Whenever, iCarly, and NCIS.

In a few short movies like A False Prophet, Morton, To Slay a Giant, and 2 Dogs Inside, Full has made appearances. Resilience, a drama/thriller also starring Henry LeBlanc, Al Rossi, Julie Alexander, Steve Wilcox, and Amy Arce, also featured him in the lead role.
