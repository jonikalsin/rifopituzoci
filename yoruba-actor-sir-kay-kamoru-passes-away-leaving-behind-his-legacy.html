Yoruba Actor Sir Kay-Kamoru Passes Away, Leaving Behind His Legacy

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, May 30, 2024

It is with deep sadness that we announce the passing of Yoruba actor, Kamal Adebayo, popularly known as Sir Kay-Kamoru.

Renowned for his distinctive language in movies, he is also the father of skitmaker Isbae U.

His demise comes shortly after being surprised by Isbae U with a N1m cash birthday gift earlier this year.

The Yoruba film community has taken to Instagram to express their grief over his departure.

The cause of his passing has not been disclosed.
