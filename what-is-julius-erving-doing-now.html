What is Julius Erving doing now?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, August 4, 2024

Julius Winfield Erving II, widely known as Dr. J, is an American former professional basketball player who has left a lasting legacy in the world of sports. Even after retiring from professional basketball, he has been involved in various endeavors, from business to philanthropy and more. Let's take a closer look at what Julius Erving is doing now, post his legendary basketball career.

Post-Retirement Accomplishments

Julius Erving retired from professional basketball in 1987 at the age of 37. However, his impact on the sport and beyond has continued to be significant. Here's a detailed look at what he has been doing since his retirement:

Business Ventures

After his basketball career, Julius Erving ventured into the business world. He obtained ownership of a Coca-Cola bottling plant in Philadelphia and also worked as a television analyst. Moreover, he joined the front office of the Orlando Magic as Vice President of RDV Sports and Executive Vice President. Additionally, he was involved in NASCAR, co-owning a NASCAR Busch Series team from 1998 to 2000, which was the first NASCAR racing team owned completely by minorities.

Philanthropy and Community Engagement

Julius Erving has been actively involved in philanthropy and community engagement. He has spoken about his Christian faith and its significance in his life. Moreover, he has been part of significant events, such as attending an unveiling ceremony at the University of Massachusetts Amherst for statues honoring himself and fellow UMass alumni and Basketball Hall of Fame members. His involvement in such events showcases his continued engagement with his community and alma mater.

Family and Personal Life

Erving's personal life has seen various milestones post-retirement. He married Turquoise Erving in 1972, and the couple had four children together. However, they faced a tragedy when their 19-year-old son Cory passed away in a car accident. Entertainment and Media Appearances

Apart from his business and philanthropic endeavors, Julius Erving has made several appearances in the entertainment and media industry. He has made cameo appearances in movies and TV shows, such as "Philadelphia," "Hangin' with Mr. Cooper," and "The Office". His diverse involvement in the entertainment industry reflects his continued presence in the public eye beyond his basketball career.

Continued Recognition in the Sports World

Despite retiring from professional basketball, Julius Erving continues to be a prominent figure in the sports world. He has shared his insights on current basketball-related topics, such as the NBA Slam Dunk Contest and the performances of contemporary players. His continued engagement in sports-related conversations demonstrates his enduring impact on the basketball community.

Julius Erving's life after retirement from professional basketball has been multifaceted and impactful. From his involvement in business and philanthropy to his continued recognition in the sports world, he has maintained a diverse and influential presence. His personal milestones and contributions to various fields reflect a life rich with experiences and endeavors beyond his legendary basketball career. As a result, Julius Erving remains a revered figure, not only in the realm of sports but also in the broader context of business, entertainment, and community engagement.
