Early Life

Grandayy was born on 1st June 1994, in Malta. He is Maltese by nationality. Currently, he is 29 years old and his zodiac sign is Gemini. He graduated from a university with a medical degree. There is no information about his family background.

Career and Professional Life

He started posting videos in 2007 on his then-main channel ‘grande1899’. He rose to fame in 2011 when he began sharing his Minecraft note-block song covers.

In 2014, he created his channel (now his main channel) and continued sharing songs on ‘grande1899’ and clips of soccer on ‘Grandayy’.

On November 1, 2016, he started uploading his ‘We Are Number One memes on his account. All these memes added to his popularity and eventually garnered him thousands of subscribers.

Two of these memes ‘We Are Number One but it’s Worked by Crash Bandicoot’ and ‘We Are Number One but it’s borked by Gabe the Dog’ are extremely popular. With over 18 million and over 10 million views, these are among the most-watched videos.

About his music career

Moreover, he started making music in 2011. Since then, he has released several Minecraft note-block songs, with a recent one being a parody of PewDiePie’s ‘Bitch Lasagna, Witch Savanna.’ All of his tracks are available on YouTube, Spotify, Deezer, iTunes, and Google Play Music.

Some of his songs are ‘Sunrise,’ an original composition featuring Tom Spander, ‘China Gang,’ a parody song employing sound clips of Donald Trump, ‘The Fall of PewDiePie,’ a diss track, and ‘China’, a parody of the track ‘Havana’, again employing sound clips of Trump.

In 2018, he was featured in PewDiePie’s version of YouTube Rewind 2018 alongside other YouTubers including Dolan Dark and FlyingKitty.

Further, In December, he collaborated with ReptileLegit to develop a parody of ‘Muffin Time,’ titled ‘Gamer Time’. Further, In 2019, he again teamed up with ReptileLegit for ‘Congratulations’, a parody using Minecraft note blocks.

He is often featured in his videos, especially in his series called ‘Meme Review’. He has collaborated with ReptileLegit to make Minecraft parodies. Likewise, he, along with Dolan Dark, has been considered “two of the most prolific creators of memes over the last decade”.

Appearance in the Minecraft

His avatar has a Minecraft note block for a head with silky, long white hair tied in a ponytail. Along with his hair, he also has a short white beard stretching across almost half his face, referencing The Witcher 3’s character Geralt, right below his yellow eyes.

His left eye can be seen having a scar across it, however, this sometimes is left out of the avatar. Moreover, his outfit is a direct reference to Robbie Rotten, a character from the TV show Lazy Town, which he had made memes of in the past.
